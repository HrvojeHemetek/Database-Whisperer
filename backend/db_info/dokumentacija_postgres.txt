-#-# Table: cards

### Description
This table stores information about bank-issued cards for users, including card type, limit, and status.

### Column Descriptions
- **card_id**: Unique identifier for the card.
- **user_id**: Identifier for the user. References `users(user_id)`.
- **card_number**: The number of the card.
- **card_type**: The type of the card (e.g., credit, debit).
- **card_limit**: The limit of the card.
- **expiration_date**: The expiration date of the card.
- **employee_id**: Identifier for the employee who issued the card. References `employees(employee_id)`.
- **status**: The status of the card (e.g., active, blocked).

### Primary Keys
- **card_id**

### Foreign Keys
- **user_id**: References `users(user_id)`.
- **employee_id**: References `employees(employee_id)`.
